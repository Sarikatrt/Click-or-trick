What is Phishing?

Phishing is a social engineering attack where attackers impersonate trusted sources to steal sensitive information such as passwords, financial data, or confidential company information.

Types of Phishing

Email Phishing

Fraudulent emails pretending to be legitimate organizations.

Spear Phishing

Targeted phishing attacks directed at specific individuals.

Smishing

Phishing conducted through SMS messages.

Vishing

Voice phishing via phone calls pretending to be banks or authorities.

Common Red Flags

  • Urgent deadlines
  • Suspicious sender email
  • Requests for personal or company data
  • Spelling or domain errors
  • Unusual payment or credential requests

How to Protect Yourself

  • Verify sender identity
  • Never share credentials via email
  • Use multi-factor authentication
  • Report suspicious messages
